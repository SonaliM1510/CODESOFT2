    W.i.S.H

    Indian Girl Group

The group's first single 'Lazeez' released on March 1 and already has over four million views on YouTube. But when I hop on a video call with Ri (Riya Duggal), Sim (Simran Duggal), Zo (Zoe Siddharth) and Suchi (Suchita Shirke) to learn about their musical journeys, they’re far from snobby celebrities in the making. We instantly digress into the shows they’re watching: a seventh go at Brooklyn Nine-Nine, reruns of Supernatural and a hearty serving of trashy reality TV. In fact, the conversation flowed so easily that my flatmate later asked if I was catching up with my high school best friends.
